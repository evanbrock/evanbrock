With Stash, you see the world differently. Imagine if the Stash app was your own personal heads-up display, with you throughout the day—guiding and helping you build wealth. The sequel to a spot we referred to as “Invest in Your World,” Stash was now a fully fledged financial platform. Investing, banking, and advice. We incorporated updated branding and worked with a really great team to create a new hero television commercial. Slick, modernized, and with heavy emphasis on Stash banking features like the Stock-Back® card, “Stash in Your World” takes a leap forward into the future of Stash.
